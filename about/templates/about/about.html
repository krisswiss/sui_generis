About us

Sui Generis is Irelands unique gift retailer offering hampers for any occasion to those who like things a little bit different. What better way to make a gift stand out?

Established in 2020, our ethos is our name meaning “of its own kind” and that is what we think about each and every person. Which is why we offer you a service to tailor your gift with luxury exclusive items to match the intended person.

Our products are sourced in the place we call home, Ireland. Here we have rooted out small businesses kind enough to share their handmade, organic and ethical creations which is the truest treasure of today.


Why Choose us?


We source from small high quality, organic and ethical food producers in Ireland. The wines we offer are sourced from boutique vineyards too! You won’t find these products on supermarket shelves.
